Tourist Attractions

Explore the beautiful destinations in Ratnagiri, Maharashtra

Ratnagiri Beach

Beautiful sandy beach with stunning sunset views and peaceful atmosphere. Perfect for relaxing and water sports.

Thibaw Palace

Historic palace of the last Burmese king, showcasing colonial architecture and rich cultural heritage.

Ganpatipule Temple

Famous temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, located by the Arabian Sea with spiritual significance.

16th-century fort offering panoramic views of the sea and surrounding areas with historical importance.
